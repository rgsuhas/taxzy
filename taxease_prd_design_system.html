01 — Product

Overview

TaxEase AI is a conversational, AI-powered tax filing platform designed to make India's tax system accessible to every citizen — not just chartered accountants.

One-line pitch

"We are building ChatGPT for taxes — an AI-powered platform that helps ordinary people understand, file, and benefit from taxes in the simplest way possible."

Problem Statement

India has ~82 million taxpayers, yet the majority find tax filing stressful, confusing, and opaque. Current systems are built for chartered accountants — not for a 22-year-old freelancer, a salaried employee, or a gig worker.

What's broken today
  • Dense jargon (TDS, 80C, Form 16, ITR)
  • Form-heavy, non-conversational UX
  • Zero refund transparency
  • No explanation of where taxes go
  • High dependency on CAs for simple filings
What we solve
  • Conversational AI guides every step
  • Jargon explained in plain English/Hinglish
  • Real-time refund tracking and marketplace
  • Tax usage visualization builds trust
  • Zero-to-filed in under 15 minutes

03 — Product

Core Features

Seven modules addressing specific gaps in today's tax filing experience. Priorities mapped to MoSCoW.

AI Conversational Tax Assistant
Step-by-step guided filing via natural language. Supports English and Hinglish. Understands context (salaried vs freelancer), explains TDS, 80C, HRA, capital gains. Uses Claude API with system prompt tuned for Indian tax context. Falls back gracefully when uncertain.
Must have
Tax Visualization Dashboard
Real-time cards showing gross income, TDS deducted, eligible deductions, taxable income, net tax payable, and expected refund. Donut chart for income breakdown. Recharts-powered with animated transitions as data updates from AI conversation.
Must have
Jargon Simplifier
Inline glossary triggered by underlined terms. Tooltip explains: "TDS = Tax Deducted at Source. Think of it as your employer paying your taxes in advance on your behalf." Covers 40+ terms. AI-powered expansion for unlisted terms.
Must have
Refund Tracker
Timeline view: Filed → Verified → Processing → Refund Initiated → Credited. Estimated date shown. Push notification simulation for demo. Color-coded progress states.
Must have
Refund Marketplace ✦ WOW feature
Time Value of Money applied to tax refunds. User with ₹30,000 expected refund can choose ₹25,000 Amazon/Flipkart/Swiggy vouchers today. Platform receives full refund later. Shows conversion rate and instant vs. wait comparison.
Must have
Tax Usage Visibility Map
After filing, animated breakdown of where user's tax rupees go: Roads (12%), Railways (8%), Healthcare (15%), Education (18%), Defence (13%), Digital India (6%), Other (28%). Personalized: "Your ₹22,000 funded 3 months of a government school teacher's salary."
Should have
Guided Tax Filing Flow
Step-by-step wizard for users who prefer structured flow over chat: Personal Info → Income Sources → Deductions → Review → File. Each screen has AI helper button. Pre-fills from chat context if available.

12 — Design

Motion Guidelines

Animations should feel purposeful and calm — never flashy. Every motion should guide attention, confirm state, or celebrate a user action.

Motion philosophy
Use Framer Motion in React. CSS transitions for static micro-interactions. Prefer ease-out for navigation and spring curves for celebration moments. Keep animation under 400ms except for celebration and data reveals.
Interactive Demo — Hover the card

spring · cubic-bezier(0.34, 1.56, 0.64, 1) · 200ms

Key Animated Moments
Dashboard number countup
When AI extracts income/TDS from chat, dashboard cards animate 0 → final value over 800ms with ease-out. Confirms data was captured and creates a moment of delight.
Refund redemption confetti
On clicking "Redeem", canvas-confetti fires in slate blue and clay. Card flips to show voucher code. Duration: 1200ms. Library: canvas-confetti.
Chat bubble entrance
Each new message slides in from bottom with opacity 0→1 over 180ms. AI typing indicator shows 3-dot pulse before response arrives. Framer Motion layout animation for smooth height changes.
Tax usage map reveal
India map SVG segments animate in staggered sequence (100ms delay each). Percentage labels count up. Full reveal takes 1.5s — slow it down to maximize emotional impact.
